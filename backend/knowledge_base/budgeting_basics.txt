Budgeting Basics

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend your money. This plan is called a budget. Creating this spending plan allows you to determine in advance whether you will have enough money to do the things you need to do or would like to do. Budgeting is simply balancing your expenses with your income. If they don’t balance and you spend more than you make, you will have a problem. Many people don’t realize they spend more than they earn and slowly sink deeper into debt every year.

Why is Budgeting Important?
1. It helps you to keep your eye on the prize.
2. It ensures you don’t spend money you don’t have.
3. It leads to a happier retirement.
4. It helps you prepare for emergencies.
5. It sheds light on bad spending habits.
